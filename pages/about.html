About Us

We are providers of professional development programs designed to augment the skills of professionals and to help organizations achieve their business goals.


Tech Tutor was founded in 2015 by IT Professionals having more than 2 decades in the industry in leadership positions.


The key philosophy we believe in is – Learning should be everyone’s right and trainings should value add not just to individuals but also to the organization at large.


With this philosophy at the foundation, we strive continuously to deliver quality trainings by qualified and certified instructors who come with years of industry expertise.
